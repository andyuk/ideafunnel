Getting real

This is a site to encourage those working on new projects to share their progress. Others can follow your progress. Get feedback on your project and turn follows into customers.

Crowd-sourcing market analysis

What are the top 5 project management tools? What are the most popular mobile platforms? Currently it's hard to find a good overview of a particular market.

My idea is to make it easy for people to describe their view of a market and make it public. This probably sounds like a stupid idea - wouldn't companies just make their own page and say they are the market leader? It's up to other users to decide how trustworthy and reliable the user and the data source is. Hopefully more trusted views of a market will float to the top so you can quickly get to the best information.
